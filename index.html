ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Daffa.

Hello! I'm a visual creator with a three-year film education background, specializing in Cinematography, Videography, and Video Editing. For me, a powerful visual work is born from the combination of camera aesthetics in the field and a sense of rhythm in the editing room. During my studies and various practical projects, I honed my visual instincts through cinematography understanding lighting, composition, and camera movement to build a story's atmosphere. Furthermore, my experience as a videographer has shaped me into an adaptable and agile creator, capturing moments in real time. I then perfect this creative process in the editing stage, weaving each clip into a cohesive, dynamic, and powerful narrative. Accustomed to using industrial camera equipment and standard post-production software, I'm always ready to bring fresh ideas and execute them into high-quality visual works.
